OUR WORK

Public Relation(PR) is about sending the right message to the right place and the right people, creating a strong bond and also targeting outreach. The main purpose of the PR subsystem is to reaching out the technology enthusiasts and keep them update about the work of our different subsystem of Team Tiburon. We make sure every hard work and efforts of the members get recognised and encouraged. We additionally, look out for support and sponsorship to keep our research and innovation going without any breach.
